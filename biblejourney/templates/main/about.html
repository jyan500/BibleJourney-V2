About BibleJourney

Our Goal

To provide a user friendly and accessible Bible Study Experience.

Current Features

  • All Books and Chapters available in the World English Bible (WEB) Translation.
  • Ability to save notes for a chapter and toggle between viewing modes for logged in users.
  • Ability to highlight verses and bookmark chapters for logged in users.
  • Verse of the Day (New International Version Translation) available through OurManna.com

If you would like to contribute, please submit a pull request at our github repository
